---
title: 章节
---

YAMLResume 提供灵活的选项来自定义简历的章节。您可以更改任何章节的标题并重排章节以最适合您的专业档案。这些自定义在您的 `resume.yml` 文件中的 `layout.sections` 配置中处理。

```yml lineNumbers title="resume.yml"
---
content:
  # ...

layout:
  sections:
    aliases:
      work: "Professional Experience"
      education: "Academic Background"
      skills: "Technical Skills"
    order:
      - work
      - education
      - awards
      - volunteer
```
